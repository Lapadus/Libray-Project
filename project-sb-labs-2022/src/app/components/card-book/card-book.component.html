On the Wings of the Wind

Set in the dramatic backdrop of the American Civil War and Reconstruction, On the Wings of the Wind, Margaret Mitchell's magnificent historical novel is an unforgettable tale of love and loss, of a nation divided by mortal passions and whose destiny will be changed. forever. But above all, it is the story of the beautiful and unscrupulous Scarlett O'Hara and the gallant adventurer Rhett Butler, two of the most famous characters in the history of Anglo-American literature from Romeo and Juliet, the heroes of Shakespeare.
On the Wings of the Wind is an exciting, lively novel that will linger in the minds of readers long after they have turned the last page.
