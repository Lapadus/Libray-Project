The most read authors of the month

Coolen Hoover

Author of young adult fiction and romance novels who published her first novel, Slammed, in January 2012

Nora Iuga

Romanian poet, writer. Her first collection of poems was published in 1968 and was called Vina nu e a mea

Harlan Coben

American writer of mystery novels and thrillers. The plots of his novels often involve the unresolved events.

James Peterson

American writer of the culinary arts. His first book, Sauces, won an award for best single subject

Anna Jacobs

English novelist who wrote more than 80 novels, the majority of which are historical.

Nora Roberts

American author of more than 225 romance novels. She writes as J. D. Robb for the in Death series.

Clive Cussler

American adventure novelist and underwater explorer. His thriller novels, featuring Dirk Pitt.

Lee Child

British author who writes thriller novels, and is best known for his Jack Reacher novel series.
